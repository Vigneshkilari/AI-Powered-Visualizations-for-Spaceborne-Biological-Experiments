About our Data

Our project focuses on visualizing data from NASA's Rodent Research missions, specifically OSD-379 and OSD-665. These datasets provide valuable insights into biological experiments conducted in space, helping scientists study the effects of microgravity on living organisms. By utilizing advanced AI-driven technologies, we transform this complex and high-dimensional data into interactive and accessible visualizations. This approach enables researchers to explore and understand the intricate details of spaceborne experiments, making the data more approachable and easier to analyze.
